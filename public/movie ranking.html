Best movies according to me

My top 3 movies of all time

Inception

A skilled thief, who steals secrets by entering people's dreams, is given a chance to erase his criminal record. He must plant an idea into a target's subconscious, but complications arise as dreams within dreams blur reality.

The Dark Knight

Batman faces his greatest psychological challenge when the Joker emerges as a new threat to Gotham. With chaos and moral dilemmas, Batman must confront his own values to save the city from descending into anarchy.

The Shawshank Redemption

Wrongly convicted banker Andy Dufresne forms a bond with fellow inmate Red. Over decades, they find solace and redemption through acts of common decency. Andy's hope and resilience lead to an unexpected, liberating conclusion.
